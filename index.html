Our Mission

At TextBridge, we believe that communication should be accessible to everyone, regardless of hearing ability or language barriers. Our mission is to simplify the lives of deaf individuals and travelers by providing cutting-edge assistive technology.

About TextBridge

TextBridge is a diploma project developed at HTL Ungargasse for the academic year 2026/27 by three passionate students dedicated to making technology more inclusive and accessible.

Under the mentorship of Dipl.-Ing. Mag. Dr. Martin Hasitschka, we're creating a solution that addresses real-world challenges faced by the deaf community and international travelers.
