Frequently Asked Questions

Is TextBridge available for purchase?

TextBridge is currently a diploma project. While not yet commercially available, we're exploring options for future development and distribution.

Can I try TextBridge?

We occasionally conduct user testing sessions. Contact us to express interest in participating in future testing opportunities.

What languages are supported?

TextBridge currently supports over 50 languages for translation, with speech-to-text available in multiple languages and expanding sign language support.

Are you looking for collaborators?

We're open to collaboration with organizations, researchers, and companies interested in accessibility technology. Reach out to discuss opportunities.
