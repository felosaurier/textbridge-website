Meet Our Team

Three passionate students from HTL Ungargasse united by a vision to make communication accessible for everyone.

The Inventors

Our diverse team brings together expertise in software development, hardware engineering, and AI to create TextBridge.

Felix Horngacher

Lead Developer & AI Specialist

Felix is the mastermind behind TextBridge's AI algorithms. With a passion for machine learning and natural language processing, he developed the speech-to-text and sign-to-text recognition systems. His expertise in Python and TensorFlow has been crucial in creating accurate and efficient models.

Responsibilities:

  • AI model development and training
  • Speech recognition implementation
  • System architecture design
  • Performance optimization
LinkedIn

Oliver Kellner

Hardware Engineer & Design Lead

Oliver brings the hardware expertise to TextBridge. He designed the ergonomic glasses frame, integrated the display and camera systems, and ensured the device is comfortable for all-day wear. His background in electrical engineering and product design makes him invaluable to the project.

Responsibilities:

  • Hardware design and prototyping
  • Display integration
  • Camera system optimization
  • Ergonomics and user comfort
LinkedIn

Raphael Klein

Software Engineer & UX Designer

Raphael focuses on the user experience and software integration. He developed the companion mobile app, designed the user interface, and implemented the translation features. His skills in full-stack development and UX design ensure TextBridge is both powerful and easy to use.

Responsibilities:

  • Mobile app development
  • User interface design
  • Translation system integration
  • User experience optimization
